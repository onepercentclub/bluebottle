Empowered by sanitary pads

India
0%
€1,587 To go 74 days A stress free mind and a disease free body, it is life’s most basic desire. With this project we include local Indian women in the production process of making sanitary pads, hence generating new livelihood opportunities. Along the way, and on the basis of these sanitary pads, health and hygiene information will be imparted by conducting health camps in the community. Prevailing attitudes toward menstruation will change. Generating job opportunities and feminine hygiene all in one: becoming empowered by sanitary pads! View project
